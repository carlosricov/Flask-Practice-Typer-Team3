About Practice Typer

Practice Typer is a time-based experience focused on honing your skills as a programmer. Put your typing speed to the test by attempting to re-type common code of the language of your choosing as fast as you can.

Practice Typer features:

  • Current support for up to 3 languages: Java, C, & Python
  • Easy-to-Use interface that's perfect for getting straight to typin' and to practicin'
  • User-accounts that keep track of your individual progress and in-practice stats

Login or Register

Access the Practice Typer experience by either logging in or creating an account. To login or register please use the buttons below. Our website is free to any aspiring or experienced programmer.
